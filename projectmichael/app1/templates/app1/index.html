Michael Onyema Edeh, PhD is a distinguished Nigerian academic that currently serves as the Deputy Dean and Head of the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at Coal City University, Enugu, Nigeria, where he formerly held the position of Director of ICT. Michael is also an Adjunct Professor at Shobhit University in India; Institute of Management and Technology (IMT) Enugu Nigeria, and Enugu State College of Education Technical, Nigeria.
He has an extensive teaching portfolio, having lectured at various institutions, including Saveetha School of Engineering in Chennai, India, Spiritan University Nigeria, National Open University of Nigeria (NOUN), Alex Ekwueme Federal University in Ebonyi State, and Gregory University in Uturu (Enugu Study centre), Pogil College of Health Technology, Ogun State Nigeria, Southwestern University, Ogun State Nigeria; African Thinkers Community of Inquiry College of Education (ATCOI-COE) Enugu Nigeria; and also Federal Science and Technical College Ogun State. He is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Research Institute of IoT Cybersecurity, National Kaohsiung University of Science and Technology, Taiwan.
His research contributions are notable, with over 3,450 citations and an h-index of 25 on Google Scholar. He has published over 130 scholarly (research) papers in reputable journals, and authored different books. Prof. Michael has been recognized for his excellence in academia, receiving the Chancellor's Award for Best Staff of the Year in 2020 and the Vice Chancellor’s Award for Research Excellence in 2023 at Coal City University., and also listed among the Top Scientists (Research Scholars) in Nigeria by Scopus (Scival) 2021-2025.
He serves as the Chairman of the Nigeria Computer Society (NCS), Enugu State Chapter, and has delivered Keynote speeches for many organizations and conferences both locally and internationally. He has acted as Guest for public Affairs Analysis on Television and radio including: Channels Television, Dream FM, Radio Nigeria and many others. His research interests include Computer science, cybersecurity, machine learning, education, and health informatics.
